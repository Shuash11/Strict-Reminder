Features

7 Reminder Categories

Every part of your routine covered — from medication to self-care, with smart features that adapt to how you respond.

💊

Medication

Track dosage, frequency, and get confirmation prompts to verify you took it.

🍎

Meal

Log breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks with optional notes.

💧

Hydration

Stay on top of your water intake with recurring reminders.

🏋️

Exercise

Set workout reminders and track completion.

😴

Sleep

Bedtime and wake-up reminders with sleep log tracking.

🧘

Self-Care

Mindfulness, breaks, and personal wellness reminders.

✏️

Custom

Create your own reminder type with a custom confirmation question.

🔔

Smart Follow-Up

If you snooze, ForReal pops right back — you pick the interval. It keeps asking until you confirm.

🚨

Escalation Alarm

After too many snoozes, the alarm escalates — persistent alert that demands your attention.

📊

History Tracking

Every response is logged. View your history to see patterns in what you complete vs. snooze.
